Sodaro

Application-Driven Solutions for a New Era

Bullimalinna Sot
Graduate Thesis Proposal
Fall of 2025

As a graduate student at CSUEB studying Interaction Design and Interactive Arts, my main focus is on developing an application tool called Sodaro. This platform helps people enter the stock market by focusing on user behavior and world news rather than traditional charts...

For my research, I’m building a “buddy” AI assistant that reads real-time data and analyzes trends based on global events. The goal is to give users real-time, calm guidance on when to buy or sell—not based on hype, but on strategy...

Sodaro encourages goal-oriented trading, capital gain management, automated account syncing, and tax optimization. It’s a personal financial tool that thinks like a human, not a spreadsheet...

As I continue refining Sodaro, I’m combining what I’ve learned from professors, startup experience, and real life. From Adobe to Figma, Miro to JavaScript, every skill is going into making Sodaro clean, powerful, and deeply human-focused...

I want Sodaro to help people from places like East Oakland, where I grew up. Many don’t get the chance to learn about finance unless they break the law. This platform gives them a legal, creative path to wealth...

Despite financial pressure, health concerns like stage 2 hypertension, and needing to stay low-key for safety, I’m determined. Sodaro is more than my thesis—it’s my life’s mission.
